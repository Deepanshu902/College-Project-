Features

How It Works

  • Expense Tracking Functionality: Both the website and app allow users to track their expenses and incomes efficiently.

  • Addition of Expenses and Incomes: Users can easily add their expenses and incomes through a user-friendly interface. They can input details such as amount, category, date, and description.

  • Total Income Display: The platform provides a clear overview of the user's total income, making it easy for them to understand their financial standing.

  • Weekly Expense Graphs: The app offers a graphical representation of expenses on a weekly basis, enabling users to visualize their spending patterns over time.

  • Categories Management: Users can categorize their expenses and incomes into different categories such as food, transportation, entertainment, etc. This categorization facilitates better organization and analysis of financial data.

  • User-friendly Interface: The interface of both the website and app is designed to be intuitive and easy to navigate, ensuring a smooth user experience for users of all levels of tech-savviness.

  • Data Security: Ensuring the security of user data is paramount. The platform implements robust security measures to protect user information, including encryption protocols and secure authentication methods.
